About PRAN-RFL Group

PRAN-RFL Group is one of the largest business organizations in Bangladesh. Since its establishment, the company has expanded across food, plastics, electronics, agriculture and retail industries.

The organization focuses on innovation, sustainability and quality. Today PRAN-RFL products are available in more than 140 countries worldwide, helping strengthen Bangladesh's reputation in international markets.

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life of people through responsible business practices, innovative products and continuous development.
